We Are Net Zero In Sri Lanka,
With A Pledge To Go Global

“Our journey is far from over. Let us continue to strive for excellence, working together to build a brighter, more sustainable future for all.”

Environmental
Sustainability

Air

We are dedicated to preserving air quality and reducing our carbon footprint, contributing to a healthier environment for all by harnessing renewable energy sources and optimizing resources.

Water

We are commited to fostering a sustainable water ecosystem by ensuring access to safe drinking water, minimizing water wastage, and maintaining zero discharge of hazardous chemicals.

Earth

We are dedicated to conserving the earth and its natural resources leading to a sustainable and abundant future by prioritizing recycling and reusing materials, while also promoting circularity.

Social
Sustainability

Provide: Care For Our Own

We strive to facilitate sustainable livelihoods and enhance the quality of life for our families and communities through initiatives focusing on water, sanitation, healthcare, and food security.

Educate : University Scholarships

We empower future generations to thrive and succeed by enabling sustainable education solutions through learning programs, scholarships, and donations for our associates, families, and communities.

Engage: The Social Impact Assessment

We prioritize continuous interaction with our communities, that allows us to better understand their needs and implement purposeful interventions, fostering meaningful connections and mutual support.

Corporate
Governance

Process Governance

We enhance transparency, accountability, and operational excellence throughout the organization by implementing robust processes and standards and ensuring uniformity and efficiency across all functions.

Risk and Compliance

We proactively manage risk at all levels, ensuring compliance and safeguarding our operations by maintaining one language and one voice across the organization to form a systematic approach to govern our SBEs and Functions.
